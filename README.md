# AI Resume Analyzer

### AI Product Management Case Study

AI Resume Analyzer is a product prototype designed to help job seekers evaluate how effectively their resume aligns with job requirements. The system analyzes resumes and generates structured feedback such as skill alignment insights, improvement suggestions, and resume optimization recommendations.

This project was developed as part of an **AI Product Management challenge** and documents the full lifecycle of building an AI-enabled product—from **problem framing and UX design to prototype development and product strategy**.

---

# Product Overview

Job seekers often struggle to determine whether their resumes effectively match the expectations of hiring managers or applicant tracking systems.

The **AI Resume Analyzer** explores how AI-driven analysis can assist candidates by automatically evaluating resumes and generating actionable feedback to improve their applications.

The goal of this project was to demonstrate how **AI product thinking** can be applied to design and prototype a practical tool for improving job application outcomes.
